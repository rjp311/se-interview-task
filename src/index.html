Are you a swimmer aged 14-25?

Swim England are thrilled to announce a new youth gala, aimed at swimmers aged between 14 and 25, to be held later in the year. This gala will be a single day event packed with a variety of races from start to finish. Details regarding races will be disclosed nearer the time. All swimmers will receive a certificate of participation in celebration of their achievements at the event.

Interested in competing?

To enter, participants must be within the ages of 14 to 25 and be associated with a Swim England affiliated club. Places are limited so early registration of interest is beneficial. We will verify your elligibility and let you know in due course! If you'd like to compete, please register by clicking the button below.

FAQs

Q: Can I compete if I am exactly 14 or 25?

A: Yes! We'd be delighted for you to get involved.

Q: Will there be galas accommodating other age groups at any point?

A: We encourage development in swimmers of all ages and competitions for swimmers outside of those specified for this event are always being considered.

Q: My club is not Swim England affiliated. Can I still compete?

A: Unfortunately, to swim at this event you will need to be associated with a club that is Swim England affiliated.
